About malGPT

malGPT is a fun, intelligent, and easy-to-use Discord bot designed to bring powerful GPT-based interactions and entertaining commands to your server.

Features

  • Slash commands for speed and ease
  • Fun tools like /8ball, /coinflip, and /roll
  • Utility features like /avatar and /ping
  • Simple setup and friendly UX
  • GPT integrations coming soon!

Who Made This?

malGPT is developed and maintained by maliinitas. Built with passion for the Discord community and a love for creating cool tools.
