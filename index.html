✅ Basic & Fun Slash Commands

  • /ping – Replies with "Pong!" to test if the bot is responsive.
  • /coinflip – Flips a coin and returns either "Heads" or "Tails".
  • /roll – Rolls a random number between 1 and 100.
  • /8ball – Answers your yes/no questions with a Magic 8-Ball style response.
  • /avatar – Shows the avatar of a mentioned user or yourself.
